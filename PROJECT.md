# SweetHome

## Tagline

**Find where life feels right.**

---

# Vision

Buying or renting a home is one of the biggest decisions people make.

Existing property platforms focus on the property itself:
price, bedrooms, floor plans and photos.

SweetHome goes one step further.

Instead of only analysing a property, SweetHome helps users understand whether that property fits the life they want to live.

By combining property information, neighbourhood insights and personal lifestyle preferences, SweetHome provides a personalised report to help users make more confident housing decisions.

---

# Problem

People often discover important issues only after moving in.

Examples include:

- Public transport is unreliable.
- The morning commute is longer than expected.
- Daily groceries are inconvenient.
- The neighbourhood doesn't fit their lifestyle.
- The property has hidden issues not obvious from the listing.

Changing homes is expensive and stressful.

SweetHome aims to reduce this uncertainty before users commit.

---

# Solution

SweetHome analyses a property together with the user's lifestyle preferences.

The platform combines:

- Property information
- Nearby amenities
- Lifestyle preferences
- AI reasoning

to produce a personalised property report.

Rather than asking:

> "Is this a good property?"

SweetHome answers:

> "Is this the right property for you?"
